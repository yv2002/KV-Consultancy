About KV Consultancy

Helping students turn their dream of studying in Italy into reality.

Who We Are

KV Consultancy was founded with a simple goal: to help students successfully pursue higher education in Italy through reliable guidance and practical support.

The inspiration behind KV Consultancy comes from real-life experience. My name is Ketan Verma, and I am currently pursuing higher education in Italy. Having personally completed every stage of the journey—including university applications, document preparation, visa procedures, accommodation arrangements, and adapting to life as an international student—I understand the challenges and opportunities that students face when planning to study abroad.

Having experienced both the challenges and opportunities of studying abroad firsthand, we understand the concerns students and parents often face. This practical experience allows us to provide personalized guidance, honest advice, and up-to-date information throughout the entire process.

What We Do

We provide comprehensive support for students planning to pursue higher education in Italy. From selecting the right university and preparing applications to visa assistance, scholarship guidance, accommodation support, and pre-departure preparation, we help students make informed decisions with confidence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the study abroad journey and provide transparent, student-focused guidance that helps aspiring students achieve their academic and career goals in Italy.
